I'm Mohammed Sulaiman — Research Engineer, MSc Physicist, and inventor. Not just a builder — an experimenter working from first principles. I start with a question nobody has cleanly answered, then design, prototype, and test until I have a device that does something new.

Work spans biomedical wearables (SpO₂, EOG, IMU rehab), medical NFC sensing, quantum computing on real IBM hardware, and environmental IoT. Every project: EasyEDA schematic → PCB → firmware → Fusion 360 enclosure → 3D print → field test.

What truly drives me is the thrill of discovering something that has never existed before. I am drawn to the deep mysteries at the frontier of physics — particularly magnetism and lasers, two of nature's most elegant and potent forces. The way magnetic fields arise from quantum spin, how coherent light bends matter to its will, and the unexplored territory where both forces converge — that is the territory I want to map through invention.
